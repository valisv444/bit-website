what is goth?

Gothic is an aesthetic and cultural style that combines darkness, melancholy and mystery. It originated in 18th century literature (Frankenstein, Dracula), moved on to art and architecture (medieval cathedrals) and later influenced music, fashion and cinema. Musically, Gothic is often associated with rock and post-punk, with dark atmospheres and introspective lyrics.
