Why did i decide to join BIT and learn and study this?

Since a few years ago I started to be more interested in the world of computers and I knew and wanted to study and be in this world. Unfortunately I couldn't study the engineering I wanted, so I discovered BIT and it seemed to me an excellent option to study and continue.

How did I hear about the BIT Institute?

While researching to study, after knowing that I was not going to be able to study the engineering I wanted, I came across BIT and it was the best option I found.

My experience

I don't really have much experience in this. So I'm starting out knowing almost nothing, but my knowledge keeps expanding and I learn a lot every time I study. And I can really see the content that to learn if I need something that we haven't seen it in class.

Expectations

To leave here being a developer with all the necessary knowledge to continue learning and working much more in this world.

My study method

I'm really a person who listens and pays attention to what is being explained to me and then puts it into practice. Maybe that's why I don't really talk at all in class because I prefer to concentrate on what I understand and once I understand, I put it into practice right away. That's why I haven't really had any problems nor do I go to the teacher for help, because the explanation is clear and concise and I proceed to write it down, understand and put it into practice. That's what helps me to learn everything correctly.
