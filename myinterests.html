Recipe for my favourite food, french fries

  1. You must first wash your potatoes. It is important to remove all the dirt impregnated in the skin of your potatoes
  2. peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  3. if you want them crispier, soak them in cold water for 30 minutes before frying.
  4. heat the oil in the fryer before putting the potatoes in.
  5. Place the potatoes in the fryer for as long as it takes for them to fry properly and become crispy and golden brown. Check them regularly
  6. Once your potatoes are fried, take them out of the oil and place them on an absorbent napkin so that any excess oil runs off and your potatoes are not too greasy.
  7. Add salt and seasoning to taste, such as paprika or pepper. Serve with your favourite sauce.
